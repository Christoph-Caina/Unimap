World Wide Astronomical Observatories Interactive Map




   With this tool you can visualize a full list of world wide astronomical observatories. This is an interactive world map with which you can get full details (photos, website, contacts, intruments, etc) of the closest observatory near to your click. I have plans in future to also add a list with all amateur astronomers observatories to date as well as allow to users to add their own observatory details and feed that back online. You can also filter or search by certain criteria.
