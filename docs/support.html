This will show you how you can identify stars and galaxies in your wide-field image (< 200mm focal length) without providing any image hint. I am also quickly going through some of the features in the software as how to overlap the sky grid, constellations artwork, measure distances between stars, view and edit image properties and check a specific sky object property.
  Cannot match your image? - then try help by specifing a hint or chose a better star catalog (Flash video format)
Alternatively you can view it in FLV video format or on youtube.com.

If your image has been shut at focal length higher then 200mm then the identification process could take a considerable amount of time in which case you can speed it up by specifying a simple hint on the position (either numeric or give an object nearby) , focal length and selecting a suitable star catalog.
