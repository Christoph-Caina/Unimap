3D Image Analysis


   This provides various analysis of the image in 3d with OpenGL. You can create a 3d graph out of your image color, map your image on top of that, 3d FFT map, 3d vigneting map and do 3d color spatial distribution. There are also two other test tools on surface and optical distotions. This tools has the full range setting for 3d graphing and viewing.
