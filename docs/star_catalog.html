UniMap software makes use of 34+ different catalogs and surveys for the plate-solving process as well as image tagging and telescope control. The user can choose to use these from either local source or remote location. It is also possible to download entire catalogs locally or either view/search the data in these catalogs manually.


Currently supported catalogs and surverys:
  • SAO Star Catalog
  • Hipparcos Input Catalogue
  • The Tycho-2 Catalogue
  • The USNO-B1.0 Catalog
  • NOMAD Catalog
  • 2MASS All-Sky Catalog
  • The Guide Star Catalogue
  • Messier Catalog
  • NGC Catalog
  • IC Catalog
  • HYPERLEDA Catalog of galaxies
  • (MCG) Morphological Catalog of Galaxies
  • (UGC) Uppsala General Catalogue of Galaxies
  • (MGC) The Millennium Galaxy Catalogue
  • MSL - Master list of radio sources
  • SPECFIND - Catalog of radio continuum spectra
  • The VLA Low-frequency Sky Survey at 74MHz
  • NVSS - The 1.4GHz NRAO VLA Sky Survey
  • ROSAT All-Sky Survey Faint Source Catalog
  • The Fourth BATSE Burst Revised Catalog
  • INTEGRAL reference catalog
  • BeppoSAX/GRBM gamma-ray Burst Catalog
  • 2XMMi - The XMM-Newton 2nd Incremental Source Catalogue
  • The HEAO A-1 X-Ray Source Catalog
  • Asiago Supernova Catalogue
  • Sternberg Supernova Catalogue
  • SDSS DR5 virial black hole masses
  • Radio and X-ray-emitting broad-line AGNs
  • The Washington Visual Double Star Catalog
  • CCDM (Catalog of Components of Double & Multiple stars)
  • The Extrasolar Planets Encyclopaedia
  • Orbits of Minor Planets
  • Database of the orbital elements of comets
  • Celestrak Satellite Catalog (SATCAT Mixed)
